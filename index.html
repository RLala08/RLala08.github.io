Kimberly-Clark Hand Towel Separation

Oct 2021 - Dec 2021

For our final project in our First-Year Engineering class, our team was tasked with designing a solution to improve the recovery of Kimberly-Clark paper towels. Our solution had to be able to separate paper towels from other trash for compost purposes. We followed the steps of the Engineering Design Process in order to generate our solutions and write multiple technical briefs documenting the process. The steps we took to create our final solution included problem scoping, idea generation, computer-aided & physical prototyping, testing, feedback iteration, and decision making. We then created a final technical report and presentation summarizing the project.
